HOW IT WORKS

Step 01

Sign up

Fill out all the required details on the signup page. Once you submit, you should get a verification email. If any issue arises contact us at customerservice@oldpals.org
Step 02

Verify

Click the link sent to you on your email, fill out the profile details as accurately as possible so we can help you find out a match with similar interests and stories!
Step 03

Training

You will receive an email for the training details. You will need to read through and fill out the attached document to complete the training. We will review your application once you’re done!
Step 04

Get matched

Congratulations! You have qualified for a match. We will send you an email with the details of your match. (we never share any information besides name and interests).
Step 05

Confirm

If you can meet your match, log onto your dashboard and click confirm, if you are not able to meet at a certain time of the day, send your match a chat asking to reschedule.
Step 06

Meet

Log in to your dashboard and click the meeting link to start the call. Finally, you’ll get to meet your match!
